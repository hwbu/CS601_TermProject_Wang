My Hometown

I was born in Taiyuan, which is Shanxi's capital, owning a long history of over 2,500 years, features with captivating cultural relics. If you ever get a change to visit there, be sure to go to the Twin Pagoda Temple, Jinci Temple, the Coal Museum of China, Pingyao Ancient City, Yingxian Wooden Pagoda. For more advise, you can check the link. Visit Taiyuan!

In Taiyuan, people's staple food is noodles. This area does not have many agricultural products, so people developed different kinds of noodles to satisfy their stomach. According to statistics, for over 2,000 years, people have used their imagination and skills to developed more than 1,000 types of flour-based dishes!
